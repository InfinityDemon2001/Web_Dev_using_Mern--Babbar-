Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Khoya (Mawa)
  • Flour (Maida)
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Baking Powder
  • Cardamom Pods
  • Lemon Juice
  • Rose Water or Saffron Strands
  • Water
  • Ghee or Oil

Recipe

  1. Crumble Khoya: Take 1 cup of crumbled khoya.
  2. Add Dry Ingredients: Mix 2 tbsp maida and 1/4 tsp baking powder.
  3. Knead: Gently knead into a smooth dough using milk.
  4. Rest: Let the dough rest for 10 minutes.
  5. Divide Dough: Make small portions.
  6. Shape Balls: Roll into smooth, crack-free balls.
  7. Boil Sugar: Boil 2 cups sugar with 1.5 cups water.
  8. Add Flavors: Add cardamom and 1 tsp rose water.
  9. Simmer: Cook syrup until slightly sticky.
  10. Add Lemon Juice: Add 1 tsp lemon juice to avoid crystallization.
  11. Heat Ghee: Heat ghee/oil on low-medium heat.
  12. Fry Balls: Fry balls evenly until golden brown.
  13. Drain: Remove and drain excess oil.
  14. Add to Syrup: Immediately immerse in warm sugar syrup.
  15. Soak: Let soak for 2-3 hours.
  16. Garnish: Optional nuts for topping.
  17. Serve: Enjoy warm or cold!

Barfi

Ingredients

  • Khoya (Mawa)
  • Ghee
  • Sugar
  • Cardamom Powder
  • Chopped Nuts (like almonds, pistachios)
  • Milk
  • Silver Leaf (Vark)

Recipe

  1. Heat ghee in a pan.
  2. Add crumbled khoya (2 cups).
  3. Cook on low flame, stirring continuously.
  4. Add sugar (1 cup).
  5. Mix and cook until sugar dissolves.
  6. Add cardamom powder (1/2 teaspoon).
  7. Grease a tray with ghee.
  8. Pour the mixture into the tray.
  9. Spread evenly with a spatula.
  10. Sprinkle chopped nuts on top.
  11. Press gently into the mixture.
  12. Cool and set at room temperature.
  13. Cut into pieces when set.
  14. Decorate with silver leaf (optional).
  15. Serve and enjoy your barfi!
